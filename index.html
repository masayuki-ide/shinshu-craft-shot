Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how SHINSHU CRAFT-SHOT (the "App") handles your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, phone number, or location data) to any external servers.

2. Data Storage

All data recorded by the user within the App (beer photos, names, dates, etc.) is stored exclusively on the user's device and on the user's own iCloud storage (for backup purposes). The developer does not have access to this data.

3. Camera and Photo Library

The App requires access to the device's camera and photo library to allow users to record their beer experiences. Images acquired through these features are used only for display within the App and storage on the device; they are never transmitted externally.

4. Disclaimer

The developer is not responsible for any trouble, loss, or damage resulting from the use of this App. Drinking and driving is prohibited by law. Drinking under the age of 20 is prohibited by law. Please enjoy alcohol responsibly.

5. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please use the link (Google Form) provided within the App.
